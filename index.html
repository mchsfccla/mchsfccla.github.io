What is FCCLA?

FCCLA's Mission: Through Family and Consumer Sciences education, FCCLA promotes personal improvement and leadership development.

Our Focus: Participate in the Hyrum Community through acts of service

Career Readiness: Prepares FCCLA Members for Careers In FCS Including:

  • Arts & Design
  • Hospitality & Tourism
  • Financial Services
  • Education & Training
  • Human Services
  • Public Service
